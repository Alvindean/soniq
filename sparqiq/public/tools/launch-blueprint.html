Frequently Asked Questions

What is a launch blueprint?

A launch blueprint is a week-by-week action plan that takes you from idea to first paying customer in 90 days. SparqIQ's AI generates one tailored to your specific idea, skills, and budget.

How detailed is the 90-day plan?

Very detailed. Each week has specific tasks, tools to use, and milestones to hit — covering validation, MVP building, launch day, and early growth.

Does it account for my budget?

Yes. You select your available budget and the blueprint recommends approaches and tools that fit — from $0 bootstrapped to $10k+ funded.

Can I use it for any type of business?

Yes. The Launch Blueprint works for SaaS, productized services, physical products, communities, info products, and more.

Is Launch Blueprint free?

Yes. SparqIQ's free tier includes 10 AI generations per month, which includes Launch Blueprint use. No credit card required.

How is this different from a business plan?

A business plan is a document for investors. A launch blueprint is an operational roadmap — specific tasks, week by week, focused on getting to revenue as fast as possible.
